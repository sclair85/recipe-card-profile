Butter Garlic Steak

The best temperature to cook steak to will depend on how you like it: Rare - 120°F, Medium Rare - 130°F, Medium - 140°F, Medium-Well - 150°F, Well Done - 160°F.

Ingredents
Quanity Ingredient Instruction
1 (¾ lb) Bonless Top Sirloin
2 tbsp Butter Divide ½
½ tsp Garlic Minced
1 tsp Fresh Parsley Minced
¼ tsp Soy Sauce
to taste Salt
to taste Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a bowl combine 1 tablespoon of butter, parley, garlic and soy sauce.
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper
  3. Over medium heat, melt remaining butter and add steak. Cook until meat is at desired doneness.
  4. Remove from heat and let steak sit for 5 - 10 minutes.
