Baked Oatmeal For One

This recipe is a baked oatmeal for one person. You can optionally add some berries or chocolate chips if you like.

Ingredents
Quanity Ingredient Instruction
¼ cup Oats
⅛ tsp Ground Cinnamon
⅛ tsp Salt
¼ cup Milk Any type
1 tbsp Salted Butter Divide ½
¼ tsp Vanilla Extract
1 tbsp Honey
1 Large Egg

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. In a 5-inch pan butter the dish with ½ tablespoon of butter.
  2. Mix together the oats, cinnamon, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. In another small bowl, whisk together the milk, ½ tablespoon melted butter, vanilla, honey, and egg.
  4. Pour mixture into the buttered pan
  5. Bake until the top is golden brown, 40-45 minutes.
