Single Serve Brownie

If you don't have self-rising flour for this recipe just mix it together before you start: 1 cup of all purpose flour, 1Â½ tsp of baking powder, and ¼ tsp salt. Use 2 tbsp for the brownie and store the rest.

Ingredents
Quanity Ingredient Instruction
2 tbsp Unsalted Butter Melted
2 tbsp Self-Rising Flour
2 tbsp Brown Sugar Packed
1 tbsp Granulated Sugar
1 tbsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1 Large Egg Yolk
1 tbsp Water
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
1 tbsp Chocolate Chips Optional
1 tbsp Walnuts Optional

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Place butter in a 6"x4" pan to melt it. Pour melted butter in a bowl and brush residual butter left in the pan
  2. In a separate bowl whisk together brown sugar, sugar, flour, and cocoa.
  3. Add the water, vanilla, egg yolk, and melted butter.
  4. If you have the optional ingredients(chocolate chips and walnuts) you can mix them in or add them on top. Pour mixture into the pan and bake for either 15 minutes for a gooey consistency or 20 minutes for a cake consistency.
  5. Remove brownie from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
