Jalapeno Dip

This jalapeno dip recipe can be prepared for potlucks or for expected guests at lunch. If you wish to make it spicier you can add 1 or 2 more jalapenos or other desired pepper. For less spice cut out the membrane and the seeds.

Ingredents
Quanity Ingredient Instruction
4 Jalapeno Diced
1 ½ cups Cheddar Cheese
454 grams Bacon Cut up and cooked
250 grams Cream Cheese Softened
1 cup Mayonase
1 cup Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a bowl mix mayonase, sour cream and cream cheese until thoroughly combined. Mix in cheddar cheese and bacon.
  3. Pour mixure into a 9"x13" pan and bake for 20 minutes or until the dip is bubbling.
  4. Cool for 5 - 10 minutes and serve with tortilla chips or your favorite crackers.
